THE ELEGANCE OF NOW, THE ATTITUDE OF THEN

A Throwback with Modern Sophistication

Drawing on the glamour of the ’80s, the design embraces a Sea Point landmark through a contemporary lens. From impeccable material choices to curated layouts and spaces, this development delivers a quiet kind of boldness – understated, yet impossible to ignore.

Inside, textured surfaces, statement tiling, and rich, natural materials create warmth and elegance. Each apartment is designed with beautiful finishes, abundant natural light, and seamless indoor-outdoor flow. From studios to penthouses, no two layouts are quite the same.

UNIQUE LAYOUTS & WIDE VARIETY

A Home for Every Story

This collection of thoughtfully designed spaces delivers on quality and individuality.

With 82 apartments ranging from studios, one and two bedroom layouts to duplexes and penthouses, Dear Jean offers something for every lifestyle. Whether you’re looking for a cosy coastal retreat or an expansive home with a private pool and large terrace, this collection of thoughtfully designed spaces delivers on quality and individuality.

With nearly every apartment including a balcony or terrace, residents will enjoy exceptional views. Many front-facing units look out to the ocean, while others boast majestic outlooks towards Lion’s Head or Signal Hill – a triple-view offering right in the heart of Sea Point. Dear Jean also offers parking with select units, plus additional available to purchase – a rare convenience in Sea Point. The building is also pet-friendly, and there are curated furniture packs available from Bone Studio and The Beautiful Room by Newman.

EVERYTHING YOU WANT

Just Steps Away

At Dear Jean, the ground and first floors are home to a hand-picked collection of retail experiences and dining. Step outside and Sea Point’s vibrant lifestyle awaits, from the iconic Promenade to stylish boutiques, artisanal cafés, and some of Cape Town’s most celebrated restaurants.

AN INVESTMENT

That Works For You

Whether you’re buying to live, invest, or both, Dear Jean offers a compelling opportunity in one of Cape Town’s most sought-after suburbs. With short-term rentals permitted and a partnership with Propr, a leading rental management company, investors can enjoy hassle-free income potential while maintaining full control of their property.

Furniture packs from Bone Studio and The Beautiful Room by Newman are also available, ensuring your unit is rental-ready from day one.

The combination of an unbeatable location, superior finishes, and flexible layouts means high demand is expected, making Dear Jean a smart addition to any portfolio.
